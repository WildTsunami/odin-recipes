Spinach Dip recipe

Description

A flavorful spinach mixture fills a tasty bread bowl.

Ingredients

  • 1 container sour cream
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/2 package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
  • 1 can water chestnuts, drained and chopped
  • 1 package dry leek soup mix
  • 1 loaf round sourdough bread

Steps

Step 1

  1. Mix sour cream, mayonnaise, spinach, water chestnuts, and dry leek soup together in a medium bowl.
  2. Chill in the refrigerator 6 hours, or overnight.

Step 2

  1. Slice off the top of the sourdough round and pull out the soft interior, leaving a sturdy bread bowl.
  2. Fill with spinach mixture.
  3. Tear removed bread chunks into pieces for dipping.
